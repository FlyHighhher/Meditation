Explore Different Meditation Techniques

Mindfulness Meditation

Mindfulness meditation involves being fully present and aware of the current moment without the judgment. It often focuses on observing thoughts, sensations and emotions as they arise.

Learn more: Mindfulness Meditation

Loving-Kindness Meditation

Loving-kindness meditation focuses on developing feelings of love, compassion, and goodwill towards oneself and others. It typically involves repeating positive affirmations and extending loving-kindness to specific individuals or groups.

Learn more: Loving-Kindness Meditation

Body Scan Meditation

Body scan mediation involves a systematic focus on different parts of the body, often starting from toes and moving upward. It aims to increase bodily awareness and release tension.

Learn more: Body Scan Meditation
